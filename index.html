TEACHING PHILOSOPHY.

1. STRUCTURAL LOGIC FIRST

We do not touch pixels until the system is mapped. You will spend the first month analyzing problems, defining user flows, and architecting information hierarchies. Aesthetics are a byproduct of structure.

2. SYSTEM OVER SCREEN

Modern products are not isolated screens; they are interconnected ecosystems. We teach scalable component architecture and design systems thinking from day one. Build for scale, not just a Dribbble shot.

3. RIGOROUS ITERATION

Your first solution is rarely the right one. We prioritize testing, data input, and breaking your own designs. Brutal feedback loops prepare you for the reality of product teams.

4. SHIP READY

Theory ends where deployment begins. The capstone is not a hypothetical case study; it is a functional product prototype, documented and ready for engineering handoff.

SYSTEM LOGISTICS (FAQS).

I WORK A 9-5. WILL THIS CLASH?

No. The schedule is engineered for working professionals. Live classes run 7:00 PM – 9:00 PM (WAT) on weekdays. If you miss a session, high-definition recordings are uploaded immediately. 60% of our cohort works full-time.

DO I NEED A POWERFUL LAPTOP?

No. Figma is a browser-based tool. If your computer can run Google Chrome with 5 tabs open, you can design. Mac or PC—it does not matter. We care about your logic, not your specs.

DO I NEED TO KNOW HOW TO CODE?

Zero coding required. "Logic-First" means we design with structure (Autolayout, Components, Variables) that translates to code, but you will not be writing lines of Javascript. We are Architects, not Builders.

IS THE SOFTWARE FREE?

Yes. We use the free tier of Figma for the entire bootcamp. You do not need to pay for any software subscriptions to complete this course. We keep overhead costs at zero.

CAN I REALLY LEARN IN 16 WEEKS?

You cannot learn everything, but you will learn competence. We cut out the fluff. No art history. No theory without practice. 16 weeks of intense, systems-based drilling is enough to make you job-ready.

WHAT HAPPENS AFTER GRADUATION?

You do not just leave; you enter the ecosystem. You gain lifetime access to the Alumni Network, bonus workshops on Rive/Framer, and direct referrals to hiring partners when available.
