Traditional design education is bloated. Four-year theories. Outdated tools. Pretty pixels without underlying logic. Vector Unleashed exists to correct this error. We build systems thinkers, not just Figma operators.

OUR MISSION.

To establish the most rigorous, logic-first baseline for product designers in Africa. We are eliminating the guesswork between "learning design" and "shipping products."

Starting January 2026, our inaugural cohort will undergo 16 weeks of high-intensity structural training. We are not focused on mass output; we are focused on engineering elite product competence.

TEACHING PHILOSOPHY.

1. STRUCTURAL LOGIC FIRST

We do not touch pixels until the system is mapped. You will spend the first month analyzing problems, defining user flows, and architecting information hierarchies. Aesthetics are a byproduct of structure.

2. SYSTEM OVER SCREEN

Modern products are not isolated screens; they are interconnected ecosystems. We teach scalable component architecture and design systems thinking from day one. Build for scale, not just a Dribbble shot.

3. RIGOROUS ITERATION

Your first solution is rarely the right one. We prioritize testing, data input, and breaking your own designs. Brutal feedback loops prepare you for the reality of product teams.

4. SHIP READY

Theory ends where deployment begins. The capstone is not a hypothetical case study; it is a functional product prototype, documented and ready for engineering handoff.

SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE (THE SYLLABUS).

16 Weeks. 4 Phases. From Logic to Launch.

PHASE 1 WEEKS 1-4

UX FOUNDATIONS & STRUCTURAL LOGIC

Deconstructing problems. User research methodologies. Information Architecture (IA). Wireframing systems. No pixels yet—only logic.

PHASE 2 WEEKS 5-8

VISUAL INTERFACE DESIGN

Typography, color theory, and grid systems. Converting wireframes to high-fidelity UI. Introduction to Figma auto-layout and component properties.

PHASE 3 WEEKS 9-12

PROTOTYPING & SYSTEMS

Advanced prototyping (variables, conditionals). Building scalable design systems. Usability testing and iterative repair loops.

PHASE 4 WEEKS 13-16

CAPSTONE & PORTFOLIO

Live client brief simulation. End-to-end product build. Case study documentation. Portfolio defense and interview prep.
