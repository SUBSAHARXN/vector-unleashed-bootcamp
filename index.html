THE REALITY.

Most bootcamps teach you how to move rectangles in Figma. They don't teach you why the rectangles are there in the first place.

At Vector Unleashed, we don't care about "pretty" until the logic works. We are building a cohort of thinkers, not just tool-users.

THE MISSION.

The tech industry doesn't need more people who can make a screen look pretty. It needs people who understand how that screen fits into a system. Starting Jan 5, 2026, we focus purely on the logic, structure, and data required to build real software.

CORE PHILOSOPHY.

1. LOGIC BEFORE PIXELS

If you can't explain the 'why', don't design the 'what'. We spend the first month purely on structure, user flows, and information architecture.

2. SYSTEMS OVER SCREENS

Isolated screens are useless. You will learn to build scalable Design Systems and component libraries that engineers can actually use.

3. FEEDBACK LOOPS

Your first draft is usually wrong. We prioritize testing and breaking your own designs so you learn how to fix them.

4. SHIP IT

Theory is nice. A portfolio is better. You will leave with a functional, documented product case study ready for review.

FAQS.

DO YOU OFFER INSTALLMENT PAYMENTS?

To ensure commitment to the cohort model, we prioritize full tuition payments. However, we offer a strict 2-part installment plan (50% to secure seat, 50% before Week 4) for select applicants. Contact Admissions to request this structure.

WHY IS THERE A SEPARATE PRICE FOR INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS?

Vector Unleashed maintains a subsidized pricing tier strictly for residents of African nations to support local talent development. Our International rate ($100 USD) reflects the global standard for mentorship of this calibre.

WILL I GET A CERTIFICATE?

Yes. Upon successful completion of Phase 4 and the submission of your Capstone Project, you will be awarded the Vector Unleashed Certificate of Product Design, verifiable on LinkedIn.

I WORK A 9-5. CAN I JOIN?

Yes. Live classes run 7:00 PM – 9:00 PM (WAT) on weekdays to accommodate working professionals. All sessions are recorded and uploaded immediately.

DO I NEED A MACBOOK?

No. Figma runs in the browser. If your laptop can handle Chrome with a few tabs open, you are good to go. Windows or Mac.

IS THERE CODING INVOLVED?

No. You will learn how design translates to code (Autolayout, Box Model), but you will not be writing Javascript or CSS.

DO I NEED TO PAY FOR FIGMA?

No. We utilize the free tier of Figma for the bootcamp. No hidden software costs.

IS 16 WEEKS ENOUGH?

You won't learn everything, but you will learn competence. We cut the fluff and focus purely on the skills that get you hired.

WHAT HAPPENS AFTER?

You join the Alumni Network. You get lifetime access to materials, bonus workshops, and referrals to our hiring partners when available.
